I am a first-year ML Ph.D. student at Georgia Institute of Technology.

My vision is to provide support for individuals and communities in advancing towards greater freedom by exploring fundamental questions. I like to develop systematic methods for solving large-scale real-world problems of broad socio-economic impact.

Research Interests

My current research interest spans deep learning, combinatorial optimization, and autonomous systems. I am particularly interested in building safe, efficient, and robust intelligent systems for mission-critical applications including autonomous driving, unmanned aircraft, cognitive robot fleet, and space robotics.
